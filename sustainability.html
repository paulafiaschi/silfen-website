sustainability

Since day one, we have launched various initiatives to keep our carbon footprint to an absolute minimum as well as making conscious decisions concerning the social impact on the communities in the countries, in which we operate.

As of 2020 we presented the brand under a new name “SILFEN”. We believe that this small change further represents a new and greener future for SILFEN. We always strive to deliver high quality products in better materials.

As a company with great manufacturing activities, we know that we have a responsibility. Our focus is to make as green a production as possible, and we will continue to launch new initiatives to lower our carbon footprint even further.

our
values

Less consumption of water in our production

We make an effort to decrease the negative impact on the environment and to ensure local access to clean water.
We introduced a rPET or Recycled Polyethylene Terephthalate. This recycled material is primarily made from plastic waste collected from the oceans.

Environmentally friendy shipping

We are in close contact with other brands in our aim to minimize shipping as much as possible – by joining forces.
We strive to ship as much as possible in bulk – mostly by sea or train. These are the most environmentally friendly modes of shipping, reducing polluting emissions.

Fair labour

It is not only the environment, we must care for, but also the people who build our products from scratch. We believe that it is our job to amplify a healthy and socially responsible conduct within our organization, including all our suppliers.
We visit our manufacturers regularly to ensure that they follow the issued principles regarding working conditions, quality, pollution etc. This allows us to promise our customers that a SILFEN bag is always crafted in a responsible manner.
